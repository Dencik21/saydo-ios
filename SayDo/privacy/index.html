Privacy Policy — SayDo

Last updated: 2026-02-28

SayDo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All voice recording, speech recognition processing, task creation, reminders, and calendar integration are performed locally on the user's device.

The app may request access to:

  • Microphone — to record voice input
  • Speech Recognition — to convert speech to text
  • Calendar — to create calendar events (optional)
  • Notifications — to send reminders (optional)

No data is shared with third parties.

If you have any questions, contact: denisilchenko21@icloud.com
